FAQ

What should I bring?

Sunscreen, a hat, comfy shoes, and your best dance moves!

Can I re‑enter the festival grounds?

Yes, with a valid wristband you may exit and re‑enter until 9 PM.

Is there parking on site?

Limited paid parking is available; we recommend car‑pooling or ride‑shares.

Are kids allowed?

Connoroo is family‑friendly—children under 10 enter free with a ticketed adult.

What items are prohibited?

Outside alcohol, weapons, pets (service animals OK), and glass containers.

Will there be food vendors?

Over 30 local vendors will keep you fueled with Florida favorites.

Can I bring a lawn chair?

Low‑profile chairs are welcome in designated chill zones.

Is the event rain or shine?

Yes! Florida weather can change quickly, so pack a poncho.

Where can I find lost & found?

Next to the main info booth—check there or email us after the fest.

Do you offer ADA accommodations?

Absolutely—accessible viewing areas, restrooms, and parking are available.
