About Connoroo

Connoroo is a magical world of music, sun, and friendship. Musical acts from around the world perform the best in indie, pop, and alternative rock. Expect bright lights, soaring guitar solos, and an unforgettable experience.

When & Where

Date: 05 JULY 2025

Time: 0800-2400

Location: Moonfox on the Hill, Madison NJ

FAQ

What should I bring?

Fresh ears, a care-free spirit, and the energy to dance!

What are the venues?

We've got 3 amazing stages providing entertainment all day long. Dance under the stars to our biggest acts at the Moonfox Stage. Chill out at the Otter Stage where we encourage you to embrace your Otter-self and lay back and crack open a cold one, the world is your oyster. At the Park stage, get some fresh air, soak up some rays, and take in the summer sunset.

Can I re‑enter the festival grounds?

Yes, with a valid wristband you may exit and re-enter

Is there parking on site?

Limited parking is available

Will there be food?

Snacks! Meals! Desserts! We'll be prepared and of course there's all that Madison has to offer

Can I bring a lawn chair?

Absolutely, though there will be chairs available at all stages. Beanbags and inflateable loungers are welcome!

Is the event rain or shine?

Yes! The Moonfox and Otter stages are covered, and the Park stage can either be moved to a nearby overhang or completely moved indoors.

Where can I find lost & found?

Next to the main info booth—check there or email us after the fest.
